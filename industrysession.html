Scope and Objectives

The session will be devoted to the applications of various intelligent methods, algorithms, and tools related to Artificial Intelligence and Big Data in industry. The aim of this session is to create a forum for exchange of ideas between the leading researchers and industry practitioners. Submissions should address practical problems and/or business applications and should be innovative. Architectures, tools, and practical implementations related to the topics described below are also of interest to this session.

Topics of Interest

Topics of interest include (but are not limited to) the following:

  • LLMs/SLMs for industry : Models and methods (RAG applied to technical documentation, multimodal models (text + sensor data + images), fine-tuning on maintenance corpora, …)
  • Applications of Intelligent Information Systems
  • Big Data
  • Cloud Computing
  • Context-aware Computing
  • Medical Applications and Bioinformatics
  • Mobile Applications and Collaborative Mobile Space
  • New Approaches to Database and Knowledge Management Systems
  • Spatial Data Analysis
  • Recommendation Systems
  • Social Networks
  • Web Technologies
  • Cybersecurity
  • Exploratory Data Analysis
  • Knowledge Discovery and Data Mining
  • Knowledge Visualization
  • Aerospace Applications

Paper Submissions

Authors are invited to submit their original unpublished papers that demonstrate current research on one of the session topics of interest. Industry session papers should be prepared in PDF using the Springer LNCS/LNAI style (10-15 pages) and submitted via ISMIS 2024 Online Submission System. All submitted papers will be reviewed by ISMIS PC members.

Publications of papers

Accepted papers will be published in the edited post-conference book published in the Springer Series Studies in Computational Intelligence.
